Our History

The history of coffee dates back to the 9th century, when it is believed that Ethiopian shepherds discovered the energizing effects of coffee beans.

From there, coffee spread to the Arab world, where it became a popular drink, and by the 17th century, it had made its way to Europe, eventually becoming a global beverage with rich cultural significance.
